Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, check if your question is answered in our tool-specific FAQ sections or below:

Are your tools really free?

Yes! All our tools are completely free to use with no hidden fees or subscriptions.

How do I report a bug?

Email us with details about the issue, including which tool you were using, your browser, and what happened.

Can I suggest a new tool?

Absolutely! We're always looking to add useful tools. Send us your suggestions!

Do you offer API access?

Currently, we don't offer API access. All tools are designed for browser use only.

How do you protect my privacy?

All our tools run entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I use these tools for commercial purposes?

Yes! Our tools are free for both personal and commercial use. Check our Terms of Service for full details.

Do you offer custom tool development?

We're always interested in expanding our tool collection. Contact us with your ideas and requirements, and we'll explore possibilities for custom development or new tool additions.
