Understanding Body Mass Index (BMI)

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a widely used screening tool that measures body fat based on height and weight. Our calculator provides instant, accurate results using the standard BMI formula.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5-24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25-29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater

Health and Fitness

Use BMI as a starting point for health discussions with healthcare providers. Remember that BMI doesn't distinguish between muscle and fat mass, so athletes may have higher BMI despite being healthy.
