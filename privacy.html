Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 20, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to ToolStation.world ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our online tools.

Information We Collect

ToolStation.world is designed with privacy as a core principle. Our tools operate entirely within your browser, ensuring maximum privacy protection:

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal files, images, or documents you process with our tools
  • Text content you enter into our tools
  • Calculation results or generated content
  • Personal identification information unless voluntarily provided

Information We May Collect

  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about page visits, browser type, and general usage patterns
  • Contact Information: Email addresses when you contact us voluntarily
  • Cookies: Small data files for website functionality and analytics
  • Technical Information: IP addresses, browser information, and device data for security and optimization

How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely for:

  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance our tools
  • Ensuring website security and preventing abuse
  • Complying with legal obligations

Data Processing and Storage

Our tools are designed for maximum privacy protection:

  • Local Processing: All tool operations happen in your browser using JavaScript
  • No Server Upload: Your files and data never leave your device
  • Temporary Storage: Data exists only in your browser's memory during tool use
  • Automatic Deletion: All data is automatically cleared when you close the browser or navigate away

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that could collect information:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous website usage statistics
  • Google AdSense: For displaying relevant advertisements
  • Content Delivery Networks: For faster website loading

These services have their own privacy policies and data collection practices. We encourage you to review their policies.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors use our website
  • Advertising Cookies: To display relevant advertisements

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Advertising

We display advertisements through Google AdSense and other advertising networks. These services may:

  • Use cookies to display personalized advertisements
  • Collect information about your browsing habits
  • Show ads based on your interests and previous website visits

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your Google Ad Settings or browser settings.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited data collection to minimize risk
  • Secure hosting infrastructure

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we may have collected
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Disable cookies through browser settings
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our website is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

International Users

Our website is accessible globally. If you are accessing our website from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Providing notice through our website or email when appropriate

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@toolstation.world
  • Contact Page: https://toolstation.world/contact.html

Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Other applicable privacy regulations
